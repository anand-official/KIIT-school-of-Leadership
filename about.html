Our Purpose

KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd)

Established in 2024 at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) DU, the KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd) nurtures future leaders by developing the qualities, skills, and knowledge needed to drive positive change. As India strives for "Viksit Bharat," KSLd cultivates leaders with technical expertise, initiative, and visionary foresight to foster change across sectors.

Leveraging KIIT DU's multidisciplinary strengths, KSLd integrates insights from business, technology, social sciences, and humanities. We develop leaders who demonstrate commitment, expertise, and passion for their work.

We believe ordinary individuals can bring about extraordinary societal change through inspired social leadership. KSLd equips learners to navigate business complexities while making decisions that benefit society and the environment. Our comprehensive approach instills a sense of responsibility to act ethically and sustainably, contributing to a more just and prosperous global community.
