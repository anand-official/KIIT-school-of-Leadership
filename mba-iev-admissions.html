Eligibility & checklist

Confirm your readiness and prepare the essentials.

Eligibility

  • Undergraduate degree from UGC recognized institutions with 10+2+3 or 4 year prior education.
  • Entrance test score (2025 onwards): CAT/XAT/MAT/GMAT/KIITEE/KIITEEM

Application checklist

  • Statement of Purpose: why MBA-IEV, what you will build.
  • Academic transcripts and entrance test scorecard.
  • Resume with projects, internships, or venture attempts.
  • Optional: letters of recommendation from mentors or employers.
