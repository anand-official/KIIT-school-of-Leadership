KIIT School of Leadership (KSLD)

We are a School of Leadership within KIIT DU. Built to be a practice-first institution for training future leaders. We provide foundational knowledge through academic studies but also provide leadership skills, soft skills, by exposure to case studies, research & development, entrepreneurship & venture building through our partnerships with the industry, startup ecosystem and research centers.

We believe leadership is practiced, not just taught. KIIT School of Leadership prepares students to be competent professionals who can think, lead and create impact.

The comprehensive approach builds a sense of purpose, ethical clarity, and the confidence to take initiative in an increasingly complex world.
