KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd)

The KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd) aims to nurture future leaders by developing key qualities, skills, and knowledge to drive positive change. With Viksit Bharat in mind, KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd) aims to nurture future leaders by developing key qualities, skills, and knowledge to drive positive change with technical expertise, initiatives, and visionary foresight across sectors. By leveraging KIIT's unique strength in multidisciplinary education, KSLd integrates insights from diverse fields, including business, technology, social sciences, and humanities. We aim to develop leaders who demonstrate commitment, expertise, and passion for their work.

At KSLd, we also believe ordinary individuals can bring about extraordinary societal change through inspired social leadership. We equips learners to navigate business complexities while making decisions that benefit society and the environment. Our comprehensive approach instills a sense of responsibility to act ethically and sustainably, contributing to a more just and prosperous global community.
