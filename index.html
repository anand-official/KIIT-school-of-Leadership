KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd)

The KIIT School of Leadership (KSLd) aims to nurture future leaders by developing key qualities, skills, and knowledge to drive positive change. As India strives for "Viksit Bharat," KSLd cultivates leaders with technical expertise, initiative, and visionary foresight to foster change across sectors.

Leveraging KIIT's unique strength in multidisciplinary education, KSLd integrates insights from diverse fields, including business, technology, social sciences, and humanities. At KSLd, we aim to develop leaders who demonstrate commitment, expertise, and passion for their work.

We believe ordinary individuals can bring about extraordinary societal change through inspired social leadership. KSLd equips learners to navigate business complexities while making decisions that benefit society and the environment. Our comprehensive approach instills a sense of responsibility to act ethically and sustainably, contributing to a more just and prosperous global community.

Social Impact

Centre for Social Research & Innovation (SRI)

Where leadership meets responsibility.

At KSLD, we believe true leadership must serve society. The SRI Centre is our hub for action-research, community projects, and policy advocacy. Students work directly with rural communities, NGOs, and government bodies to solve real-world challenges.

  • Action Research: Field-based studies on livelihood, education, and health.
  • Community Immersion: Every student spends time living and working in a rural setting.
  • Policy Advocacy: Translating grassroots insights into policy recommendations.
