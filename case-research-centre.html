Vision & Mission

A globally recognized hub for case-based research that bridges scholarship, policy, and classroom impact.

Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in case-based research, fostering innovation, influencing policy, and enriching educational experiences through rigorous and impactful scholarly inquiry.

Mission

  • Advance knowledge and practice through the development, dissemination, and application of high-quality case studies.
  • Promote academic excellence with rigorous case-based research that informs best practices in the teaching–learning process.
  • Enhance educational impact by integrating cases into curricula so learners build analytical and leadership capabilities.
  • Foster industry collaboration to co-create value, address real-world challenges, and promote innovation.
  • Drive thought leadership through publications and dialogue among academia, industry, and policy makers.

Activities

Co-creating cases, strengthening pedagogy, and convening academia–industry exchange.

Collaborative Case Development

Engaging faculty, industry experts, and alumni to build cases reflecting current business and societal challenges.

Workshops & Training

Seminars on case writing, teaching methodologies, and effective classroom deployment.

Forums & Conferences

Events bringing academia and industry leaders together to share insights and foster research collaborations.

Competitions & Experiential Learning

Case competitions and live projects where students apply skills and receive practitioner feedback.
